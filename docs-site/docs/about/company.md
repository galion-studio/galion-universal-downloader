# About Galion

> **"Your Only Limit Is Your Imagination"**

## Who We Are

**Galion** is a technology company building the future of AI-powered tools and services. We believe that powerful software should be free, open-source, and accessible to everyone.

### Our Mission

To create tools that empower creators, developers, and anyone who dreams of building something amazing. We remove barriers and make technology work for everyone.

### Our Vision

A world where:
- **AI is accessible** - Not locked behind expensive subscriptions
- **Data is free** - Your data belongs to you
- **Tools are open** - Anyone can contribute and improve
- **Imagination is the only limit** - We build what you dream

## The Galion Team

We're a passionate team of developers, designers, and AI enthusiasts who believe in the power of open source.

### Core Values

1. **Open Source First** - All our tools are MIT licensed
2. **Privacy by Design** - Your data never leaves your machine
3. **Community Driven** - Built with and for the community
4. **Quality Over Quantity** - We focus on doing things right

## Open Source Philosophy

We believe that the best software is built in the open. Here's why we're fully open source:

1. **Transparency** - See exactly how our tools work
2. **Trust** - No hidden data collection or tracking
3. **Community** - Anyone can contribute improvements
4. **Learning** - Our code helps others learn
5. **Longevity** - Open source lives forever
